# 📱 BusyApp

A fake phone call simulator that mimics a Samsung-style incoming and active call screen, complete with swipe-to-answer/decline, ringtone, and in-call controls. Perfect for pranking friends or practicing call-handling gestures without risking your social life.

## 💡 Project Description
A realistic-looking fake call interface designed for fun and harmless mischief. Features swipe-to-answer/decline gestures, contact name randomization, in-call control panel, and PWA compatibility.  

---

## ❓ The Problem (that doesn’t exist)
You’re in an awkward conversation, a boring meeting, or just bored, but nobody ever calls you at the perfect moment to save you.

---

## 🚀 The Solution (that nobody asked for)
A fake call app that *will* call you exactly when you want, with famous “contacts” like Elon Musk, Narendra Modi, and Cristiano Ronaldo—complete with Samsung-inspired animations.
